MedConnect is an Electronic Health Records(EHR) System integrated with an Appointment Management System to keep track of appointments and access your medical records all in one place. Appointment Management

Simplified appointment scheduling and management for both patients and doctors. Effortlessly browse available sessions and book appointments.

Store Medical Records

Store medical history, examination data, test results, diagnoses and treatments in a centralized digital repository and access your records anytime, anywhere.

Streamlined Appointment Process

Accelerate appointments with instant medical history. Your doctors can access your complete medical history in one place.

Medication and Treatment Plans

Keep track of medication and treatment plans effortlessly. Access your current medications, dosages, and instructions in one place.
